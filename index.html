Expert PLC Programming & Control Systems

HertzInnovation delivers comprehensive programmable logic controller programming services that enhance industrial automation and process control across diverse manufacturing environments. Our certified engineers specialize in multiple PLC platforms including Rockwell Allen-Bradley, Siemens, and Schneider Electric systems.

  • Custom PLC programming for complex industrial processes
  • HMI (Human-Machine Interface) development and integration
  • SCADA system implementation and configuration
  • Factory acceptance testing and commissioning
  • Legacy system upgrades and modernization
Industries Served: Manufacturing, Oil & Gas, Water Treatment, Food & Beverage, Pharmaceutical, Automotive

Advanced Power Distribution & Control Panel Solutions

Our power distribution and control panel services provide comprehensive electrical design, manufacturing, and integration solutions for industrial facilities. HertzInnovation specializes in creating custom control panels that meet the highest safety and performance standards while ensuring seamless integration with existing systems.

  • Custom control panel design and manufacturing
  • Motor control centers (MCCs) and switchgear solutions
  • Variable frequency drive (VFD) integration
  • Power quality analysis and improvement
  • UL-listed panel fabrication
Standards Compliance: UL, NEMA, NEC, IEEE, and industry-specific requirements
